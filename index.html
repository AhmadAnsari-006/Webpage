Explore Bhopal

A City Like No Other

Ancient heritage and modern energy in one of India's most liveable cities.

🕌

Heritage & History

From the magnificent Taj-ul-Masajid to the Bhimbetka Rock Shelters — Bhopal wears its history proudly.

🌊

Lakes & Nature

30+ lakes, the Van Vihar National Park, and lush green landscapes make Bhopal a nature lover's haven.

🍛

Food Culture

Bhopal's street food is legendary — seekh kebabs, poha, bhutte ka kees, and the iconic bhopali gosht korma.

🎨

Art & Culture

The Bharat Bhavan cultural complex, Bhojpur temple, and a thriving indie arts scene define Bhopal's creative soul.

🎓

Education Hub

IIT Bhopal, NIT, AIIMS, and dozens of premier institutions make it a dynamic city for students.

🤝

Community First

Warm, welcoming people, tight-knit communities, and a culture of belonging that lives up to every promise.

Questions

Things People Ask

AIESEC in Bhopal is the local chapter of the world's largest youth-run organisation. It connects young people with international internship and volunteer opportunities, while also hosting incoming exchange participants in Bhopal. Join to develop leadership skills, experience global cultures, and build a lifelong network.
Browse the EP Packages collection on Google Drive, then connect with the AIESEC Bhopal team through Instagram or the search tool. You'll get guidance on which programme fits your background, how to apply, and what to expect during your exchange.
Bhopal is one of India's most affordable major cities. A comfortable lifestyle, including accommodation, food, transport, and leisure, can typically be maintained for ₹12,000–₹20,000 per month. Our Cost Booklet breaks this down in detail with real data.
Hindi is the primary language, and English is widely spoken among students, professionals, and in business contexts. The AIESEC community is international and communicates primarily in English, so newcomers are always welcome.
Yes, Bhopal is widely regarded as one of the safer and more welcoming cities in central India. The community is known for hospitality, and the AIESEC team provides on-ground support throughout your stay to ensure a smooth and safe experience.
